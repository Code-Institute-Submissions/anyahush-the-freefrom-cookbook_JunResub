Top Tips

  • Dairy products such as butter and milk can be replaced with dairy free alternatives
  • For most recipes, pasta and bread can be replaced with gluten, lupin and nut or seed free alternatives
  • Not all recipes using wheat flour can be replaced like for like with gluten free alternatives
  • When using processed stock cubes, sauces, condiments etc. check for ALL allergens as you will find some where you least expect it!

Things to Remember

  • Any of the 14 major allergens will be in bold on packaging in the UK
  • If cooking for someone else with an allergy or intollerance check what they are allergic to and their severity. Some people cannot eat products with may contain labels on the ingredients
  • ALWAYS check ingredients of any processed product you are using
  • We have only covered the 14 major allergens here but people are allergic to a lot more!
