About

Welcome to The FreeFrom CookBook!

Here we want to help make cooking easier for those with food allergies. Cooking, eating out and sharing meals can often be difficult or stressful for those allergies. Not sure if 100% safe and trusting that someone else has understood fully.

We wanted to create a place that anyone with an allergy can find, friends and family, those who work in hospitality or people exploring and curious can and learn, get recipe ideas and add to our ever growing library! We want to change the perception that just because something is gluten or lactose free, for example, it doesn't have to be dull, tasteless or uninspiring.

Of course there is a safety aspect to this too. Food allergies can be fatal for some and with high levels of processed foods and condiments used these days, we want to help people learn what to look out for.

Top Tips

  • Dairy products such as butter and milk can be replaced with dairy free alternatives
  • For most recipes, pasta and bread can be replaced with gluten, lupin and nut or seed free alternatives
  • Not all recipes using wheat flour can be replaced like for like with gluten free alternatives
  • When using processed stock cubes, sauces, condiments etc. check for ALL allergens as you will find some where you least expect it!

Don't Forget

  • Any of the 14 major allergens will be in bold on packaging in the UK
  • If cooking for someone else with an allergy or intollerance check what they are allergic to and their severity. Some people cannot eat products with may contain labels on the ingredients
  • ALWAYS check ingredients of any processed product you are using
  • We have only covered the 14 major allergens here but people are allergic to a lot more!
